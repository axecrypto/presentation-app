Quick Export Instructions:

  1. Make sure the presentation app is running at http://localhost:3000
  2. Navigate to the LerBee pitch deck
  3. Click the "Generate PDF" button below
  4. A new window will open with all slides
  5. Use Cmd+P (Mac) or Ctrl+P (Windows) to print
  6. Choose "Save as PDF" as the destination
  7. Settings: Landscape, A4, No margins, Background graphics ON
